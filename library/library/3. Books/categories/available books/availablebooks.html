EDUCATIONAL BOOKS

ENGINEERING MATHEMATICS
-JHON BIRD-
7th edition
▾
A practical introduction to the core mathematics required for engineering study and practice. John Bird's approach is based on worked examples and interactive problems. This makes it ideal for students from a wide range of academic backgrounds as the student can work through the material at their own pace. Mathematical theories are explained in a straightforward manner, being supported by practical engineering examples and applications in order to ensure that readers can relate theory to practice. The extensive and thorough topic coverage makes this an ideal text for a range of Level 2 and 3 engineering courses.
