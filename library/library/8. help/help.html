Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow a book?

Students need to visit the library in person to borrow a book. Once you select your desired book, the librarian will record your details in the system. You can borrow the book for two weeks. If needed, you can renew it for an additional two weeks before the due date.

What happens if I return a book late?

A fine of 10 LKR will be applied for each day past the due date.

Can I renew a borrowed book?

You can renew a book twice before its due date by meeting a librarian.

How do I contact the librarian?

You can use the contact details in the home page or visit the library during working hours.
